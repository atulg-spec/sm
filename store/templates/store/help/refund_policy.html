Valid Refund Reasons

Technical Issues

  • Corrupted Files: Product files are damaged or unreadable
  • Wrong Format: Files don't match the advertised format
  • Missing Components: Product is incomplete or missing promised files
  • Compatibility Issues: Product doesn't work with specified software versions
  • Installation Problems: Cannot install or use the product as described

Product Quality

  • Not as Described: Product doesn't match the listing description
  • Poor Quality: Product quality is significantly below reasonable expectations
  • Outdated Content: Product contains outdated or incorrect information
  • Broken Links/Assets: Product contains non-functional elements

Licensing & Legal

  • Copyright Issues: Product infringes on third-party copyrights
  • License Restrictions: Product has more restrictive licensing than advertised
  • Usage Limitations: Cannot use product for intended purpose due to restrictions

Other Valid Reasons

  • Duplicate Purchase: Accidentally purchased the same product twice
  • Wrong Product: Purchased incorrect item due to misleading description
  • System Requirements: Product requires unsupported software/hardware

What Happens During Refund Review

Approved Refunds

  • Full Refund: Complete purchase amount returned
  • Access Revoked: Download access disabled
  • Email Confirmation: Refund confirmation sent
  • Processing Time: 3-5 business days to your account
  • Order Status: Marked as refunded

Alternative Solutions

  • Partial Refund: Pro-rated amount for used portions
  • Product Replacement: Updated or corrected version
  • Store Credit: Credit for future purchases
  • Discount Code: For similar products
  • Support Assistance: Help resolving the issue

Important Notes

Digital Product Nature

Since digital products are instantly accessible, we ask that you contact us promptly if you encounter issues rather than using the product extensively before requesting a refund.

Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed back to your original payment method. Processing times vary by payment provider and may take 3-10 business days to appear in your account.

Multiple Refund Requests

Only one refund request per order is allowed. If you've already received a refund for an order, additional refund requests for the same order will not be considered.
