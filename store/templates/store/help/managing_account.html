Your Account Dashboard

Your StartMarket account gives you access to a comprehensive dashboard where you can manage your profile, track orders, download products, and customize your experience. Let's explore all the features available to you.

Quick Access

Access your account anytime by clicking your username in the top navigation bar.

Account Management Features

Profile Settings

Customize your account information and preferences.

Basic Information

  • Display Name: Change how your name appears
  • Email Address: Update your login email
  • Profile Picture: Upload a custom avatar
  • Bio: Add a short description

Communication

  • Email Preferences: Control notifications
  • Newsletter: Subscribe/unsubscribe
  • Marketing Emails: Opt-in/out of promotions
  • Language: Choose interface language

How to Update Your Profile:

  1. Click your username in the top navigation
  2. Select "Profile Settings" from the dropdown
  3. Make your changes and click "Save Changes"
  4. Some changes may require email verification

Order History & Tracking

View all your past purchases and track order status.

Order Information

  • Order Number: Unique identifier for tracking
  • Purchase Date: When the order was placed
  • Order Status: Processing, completed, or refunded
  • Total Amount: Final price including taxes
  • Payment Method: How you paid

Order Actions

  • Download Products: Access your digital files
  • View Invoice: Download receipt
  • Request Refund: If eligible
  • Contact Support: Get help with order
  • Leave Review: Rate your purchase

Accessing Order History:

  1. Click your username in the top navigation
  2. Select "My Orders" from the dropdown menu
  3. Browse your order history or use search/filters
  4. Click on any order to view details and download products

Saved Payment Methods

Store payment methods for faster checkout (when available).

Payment Method Management

  • Add Cards: Save credit/debit cards for future purchases
  • Default Method: Set your preferred payment option
  • Security: Cards are tokenized and never stored in full
  • Remove Methods: Delete saved payment information anytime
  • Billing Address: Update associated billing information

Notification Preferences

Control what emails and notifications you receive.

Email Notifications

  • Order Confirmations: Receipt emails
  • Download Links: Product access emails
  • Updates: Account and product updates
  • Promotions: Sales and special offers
  • Security Alerts: Account security notifications

Notification Types

  • Instant: Immediate notifications
  • Daily Digest: Grouped daily updates
  • Weekly Summary: Weekly activity reports
  • Important Only: Critical updates only
  • None: Disable all notifications

Account Deletion

Permanently Delete Your Account

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, orders, and downloads will be removed.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account:

  • ‚Ä¢ All personal information is permanently removed
  • ‚Ä¢ Order history and download access is lost
  • ‚Ä¢ Email subscriptions are cancelled
  • ‚Ä¢ Reviews and comments remain (anonymized)
  • ‚Ä¢ Refund eligibility may be affected

Important Considerations

Before deleting your account, download all your purchased products and save important order information.

To delete your account, please contact our support team with your account details. We'll assist you with the deletion process.
