Payment FAQs

Is my payment information stored?

No, we never store your full credit card details or payment information on our servers. All sensitive data is handled by PCI DSS compliant payment processors and immediately discarded after processing.

What if my payment fails?

Payment failures can occur due to insufficient funds, expired cards, or bank restrictions. Try a different payment method or contact your bank. Your cart contents will be saved for 24 hours.

Can I change my payment method after checkout?

Once an order is placed, the payment method cannot be changed. If you need to modify your payment, please contact support immediately before the payment is processed.

Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees! The price you see is the final amount including all applicable taxes. We don't charge processing fees, handling fees, or any other additional costs.

What currencies are accepted?

We accept payments in Indian Rupees (INR). International customers may use payment methods that support INR transactions. Currency conversion fees may apply depending on your bank.
